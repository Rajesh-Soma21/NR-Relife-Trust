About NR Relife Foundation

NR ReLife Foundation is a dedicated rehabilitation and recovery center committed to helping individuals overcome addiction and mental health challenges. Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and structured environment where individuals struggling with substance abuse, behavioral addictions, and psychological disorders can heal and rebuild their lives.

At NR ReLife Foundation, we believe that recovery is a journey that requires holistic care. Our approach combines medical detox, therapy, counseling, and wellness programs to ensure a long-lasting and sustainable recovery. We focus on addressing the root causes of addiction and mental health issues through personalized treatment plans that cater to the unique needs of each individual.
