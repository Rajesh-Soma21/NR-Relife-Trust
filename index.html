About NR Relife Trust

NR Relife Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering a compassionate society. Established with a vision to serve the most vulnerable, we believe in holistic development and sustainable impact.

Our journey began with a focus on rehabilitation, providing a second chance to individuals battling various challenges. As our mission evolved, we recognized the pressing need for animal welfare, leading to the inception of our Goshala project. Looking ahead, we are committed to providing a dignified living space for our elders through our planned Old Age Home.

We operate with utmost transparency (LEI: KA/2025/0550409) and are driven by a team of dedicated professionals and passionate volunteers. Join us in our endeavor to create a society where everyone receives the care, respect, and resources they need to thrive.

Our Rehabilitation Center

Our dedicated rehabilitation services are provided through our experienced team, offering comprehensive programs aimed at recovery, holistic healing, and reintegration into society.

A Path to Recovery at NR Relife Trust

At NR Relife Trust, we offer a safe, supportive, and structured environment for individuals seeking a new beginning. Our programs include de-addiction therapies, mental health counseling, skill development, and post-rehabilitation support.

We believe in empowering individuals with the tools and confidence to lead fulfilling lives. Our team of experienced doctors, therapists, and counselors provides personalized care tailored to each resident's journey.

Our Upcoming Goshala (Cattle Sanctuary)

Our upcoming goshala will provide a safe haven and respectful environment for rescued, abandoned, and elderly cows. We believe in the ethical treatment and welfare of these revered animals.

Ethical & Compassionate Care

We ensure nutritious feed, regular veterinary check-ups, and a spacious, clean environment for all our cattle.

Promoting Organic Farming

Utilizing cow dung and urine for natural farming practices, contributing to sustainable agriculture.

Adopt a Cow Program

An opportunity for individuals and families to contribute directly to the well-being of a cow.

Learn More About Goshala

Our Goshala is designed to be a sanctuary where cows can live out their lives peacefully. We aim to protect indigenous breeds and promote the traditional reverence for cows. The facility will include open grazing areas, well-ventilated sheds, and an on-site veterinary clinic. We plan to educate the community on the benefits of sustainable agriculture practices utilizing natural fertilizers derived from the goshala. We welcome volunteers interested in animal welfare to join our efforts.

Upcoming: Old Age Home

We are diligently working towards establishing a compassionate and comfortable old age home, providing a safe and respectful living environment for senior citizens.

Dignified & Respectful Living

Focus on respect, comfort, and personalized care, ensuring a high quality of life for our elders.

Comprehensive Medical & Wellness

Regular health check-ups, medication management, and tailored wellness programs.

Engaging Social Activities

Promoting social interaction and mental stimulation through various recreational activities.

Learn More About Old Age Home Plans

The upcoming Old Age Home at NR Relife Trust aims to be a home away from home for our seniors. We envision a community where elders can live with dignity, engage in meaningful activities, and receive excellent medical and palliative care. Our plans include comfortable residential units, a common dining area, recreational spaces, a library, and a prayer hall. We will also facilitate visits from family members and organize intergenerational programs to keep our residents connected to the wider community. Your support is crucial in bringing this vision to life.
